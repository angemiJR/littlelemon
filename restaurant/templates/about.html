About Little Lemon

Little Lemon is a family-owned Mediterranean restaurant where tradition meets modern flavors. Our passion lies in creating dishes that are simple, fresh, and full of character, inspired by recipes passed down through generations.

We carefully select seasonal ingredients and work closely with trusted suppliers to ensure every plate reflects quality and authenticity. From handmade pasta to vibrant salads and thoughtfully prepared main courses, our food is designed to be enjoyed slowly and shared with others.

At Little Lemon, we believe that dining is more than just a meal — it’s an experience. We focus on creating a relaxed and welcoming environment where guests can feel comfortable, connect, and enjoy good company.

Our team is driven by a love for hospitality and attention to detail. Whether you are visiting for a quick lunch, a family dinner, or a special celebration, we aim to make every visit memorable.

Little Lemon is a place where flavors are honest, the atmosphere is warm, and everyone is welcome.
